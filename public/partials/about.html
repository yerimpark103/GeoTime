About this project

The Geographic Timeline Project was conceived by Stephen Cartwright as an extension of his self-tracking art projects.
For more information, visit: Stephen's Webpage.

The Geographic Timeline Project is a public interactive artwork that examines the movement of people over time.
Individual lines will accumulate to for a sprawling network of people’s trajectories through life.

To Participate:

Input – some information about where you have lived and when you have moved
See – the results of your path through time and space in a dynamic 3D visualization
Save – Save your line to the growing database
Search and Filter – once you have made your line see how it compares to others

Original web coding and design by Yerim Park.
